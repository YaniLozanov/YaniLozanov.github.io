Car Service Management System

(Latest Project)

This is my last project and also the most endeavouring one. It can give a clear vision about my level of skills untill now. Currently, some of its features are being completed, but this is not an obstacle to be considered. I have made this project in order to improve my programming skills. This application is a system for auto services, in which auto services can enrol their customers and their vehicles. The auto services also can record in details, what manipulations have been made on the car, when and where. The system also offers easy way to find customers, that are already in the system and their vehicles or just searching for a particular car. The recorded data of a particular car would be very valuable on a subsequent visit, because it will be much more easier to detect a problem and make it easier to clarify issues connected with the warranty of a particular repair.
